================================================================================
STATUS ICON LEGEND
================================================================================

    ⚠  Unlinked    - Call not linked to any package (needs attention)
    ◐  Review      - Linked but needs review/notes
    ✓  Complete    - Fully reviewed and logged
    ⊘  Spam        - Marked as spam/irrelevant
    ◷  Snoozed     - Temporarily hidden, will resurface

    ●  Selected    - Currently focused/selected call
    ○  Unselected  - Available for selection
    ☑  Multi-sel   - Part of multi-selection batch
