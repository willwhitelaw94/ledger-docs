Need Description

What is the need?

Margaret requires support with meal preparation due to reduced mobility in her left hand following a stroke in 2023. Modified texture diet (soft foods) as per speech pathologist recommendation dated 15 Nov 2025. Daughter (Susan) supplements with weekend meals and monitors fluid intake on Saturdays and Sundays. Hydration tracking chart placed on fridge door.
