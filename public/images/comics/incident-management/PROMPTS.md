# DALL-E Prompts for Incident Management Comic Strip

Use these prompts in ChatGPT (DALL-E) to generate the 4 panels. Ask for **square 1024x1024** images.

## Style Prompt (prefix all panels with this)

> Flat vector illustration in a clean modern comic book style. Soft pastel colours with teal (#007F7E) and navy (#2C4C79) accents. Simple clean lines, no heavy outlines. Aged care / healthcare setting. Characters are diverse Australian adults. Minimal background detail. No text or speech bubbles.

---

## Panel 1 — Discovery (panel-1-discovery.png)

> A female care worker in a teal polo shirt kneels beside an elderly woman who has fallen in her living room. The care worker looks concerned but calm. A walking frame is tipped over nearby. Warm home setting with a couch and rug visible. Soft red/pink tint to the scene suggesting urgency.

---

## Panel 2 — The Old Way (panel-2-old-way.png)

> Split composition: on the left, the same care worker walks away from the elderly client (who sits alone looking worried). On the right, the care worker sits frustrated at a desktop computer, staring at a complex CRM form with many fields. A clock on the wall shows time passing. Orange/amber tint suggesting frustration and wasted time.

---

## Panel 3 — The New Way (panel-3-new-way.png)

> The care worker sits next to the elderly client on a couch, holding a tablet. The tablet screen shows a simple clean form with a teal header. The client looks reassured. The care worker is smiling confidently. Teal/mint tint suggesting calm and efficiency. A small checkmark icon floats near the tablet.

---

## Panel 4 — Resolution (panel-4-resolution.png)

> Split composition: on the left, a clinical team member at a modern desk views a dashboard showing an incident alert with a red severity badge and a trend chart showing "3 falls". On the right, the care worker is helping the elderly client into bed, both looking calm and safe. Green tint suggesting resolution and care. A small heart icon floats above.
