Cherry-Pick — Select Patterns to Adopt

Click cards to vote. Copy your selections when done.

Maya Job Dashboard

Minimal public dashboard layout

Single-column, no-login experience with 1px progress bar and phase cost breakdown. Low noise, high trust — ideal first impression for homeowners.

Dan Job Dashboard

KPI summary tiles

Budget spent, remaining, and % complete as scannable metric tiles. Gives clients the key numbers instantly without reading prose.

Dan Registration CTA

Sticky bottom registration banner

Persistent but unobtrusive CTA bar at the bottom of the job dashboard. Always visible without interrupting the content reading experience.

Tim Registration CTA

Contextual "this is your spend" hook

CTA appears inline after the spend section with a direct message: "This is your $68,500 spend — want to track it?" Higher intent, lower friction.

Maya Onboarding

3-step wizard with context-aware pre-selection

"Home Renovation" pre-selected with "Came from Harrington Builders' portal" context note. Reduces decisions and reinforces the viral referral origin.

Tim Onboarding

Conversational one-question-at-a-time flow

Progressive disclosure — each question appears after the last answer. Feels fast and personal, reduces cognitive load vs seeing all fields at once.

Morgan Responsive Layout

Mobile-first responsive layout

Large touch targets, bottom-anchored CTAs, thumb-friendly navigation. Most clients will open the job link on mobile — this should be the default layout.

Dan Registration CTA

Personal vs Business split screen

Two-card choice between "Track my home project" and "Run my trade business" — captures both the homeowner AND the contractor as potential new users.
