# Business: Customizable Dashboard (Business Overview)

**Epic**: 016-CDB
**Created**: 2026-03-14
**Status**: Draft

## Executive Summary

The customizable dashboard adds a widget personalization layer on top of the existing Business Overview. Users can show/hide widgets and reorder them to match their role and priorities. The primary driver is competitive parity with Xero, available to all subscription plans.

## Business Problem

- **Current state**: The dashboard is a fixed layout — every user sees the same widgets in the same order regardless of role or priority.
- **Pain point**: Users comparing MoneyQuest to Xero expect dashboard customisation as a baseline capability. Its absence is a friction point during sales and a churn risk for existing users.
- **Opportunity**: Matching Xero's "Edit homepage" experience removes a competitive gap and increases daily dashboard engagement.

## Business Objectives

- **Primary**: Achieve feature parity with Xero's customisable dashboard to remove it as a competitive objection
- **Secondary**: Increase daily dashboard engagement by giving users a layout that reflects their actual priorities
- **Non-goals**: Building a fully programmable widget system with custom data sources or third-party widgets

## Success Metrics & KPIs

| Metric | Baseline | Target | Measurement |
|--------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Dashboard customisation adoption | 0% | >40% of active users customise within 30 days of launch | % of users who save at least one layout change |
| Competitive objection rate | Unknown | Reduction in "Xero has X, you don't" during sales | Sales team feedback / deal notes |
| Dashboard session length | Baseline at launch | Neutral or improved (no regression) | Analytics |

## Market Context

- **Competitive landscape**: Xero's "Edit homepage" is the reference — bank accounts individually togglable, widget show/hide modal, drag-to-reorder, layout saved per user
- **Timing**: Table-stakes feature for any serious accounting SaaS. No urgency beyond competitive positioning.
