Employment Hero is our HR and people management platform, handling everything from recruitment to payroll to performance management.

---

## What We Use It For

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Recruitment** | Job postings, applicant tracking, hiring workflows |
| **Onboarding** | New employee setup, contracts, documentation |
| **Payroll** | Salary processing, tax, superannuation |
| **Leave Management** | Annual leave, sick leave, other leave types |
| **Performance** | Reviews, goals, feedback cycles |
| **HR Records** | Employee data, contracts, compliance documents |

---

## Key Features

### For Employees
- Self-service leave requests
- Payslip access
- Personal details management
- Goal tracking

### For Managers
- Team leave approvals
- Performance review tools
- Onboarding checklists
- Reporting

### For HR / People & Culture
- Centralised employee records
- Compliance tracking
- Recruitment pipeline
- Analytics and reporting

---

## Integration Points

Employment Hero connects with:

| System | Integration |
|--------|-------------|
| **Xero** | Payroll and financial sync |
| **Office 365** | Single sign-on, calendar |
| **Bank feeds** | Payroll disbursement |
