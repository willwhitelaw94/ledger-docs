[Microsoft 365](https://www.microsoft.com/en-au/microsoft-365) is our primary productivity suite for email, documents, and team collaboration.

---

## What We Use

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Outlook** | Email and calendar |
| **Teams** | Video calls, chat, channels |
| **SharePoint** | Document storage and collaboration |
| **OneDrive** | Personal cloud storage |
| **Word/Excel/PowerPoint** | Document creation |

---

## Our Usage

- **Email**: All company email through Outlook
- **Meetings**: Teams for video conferencing
- **Documents**: SharePoint for shared team documents
- **Chat**: Teams channels for squad communication
